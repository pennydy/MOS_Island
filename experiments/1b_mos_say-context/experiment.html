Instruction: In this experiment, we ask you to imagine that you are at a party. You walk into the kitchen and overhear a conversation, during which one person, Scott, asked about something another person, Hanako, just said.

You are asked either to assess how natural/acceptable Scott's question sounds or to select what Hanako was talking about.

There are 4 practice trials followed by a total of 36 pairs of questions.

Payment: You will be paid $2.50 to participate in this experiment, which will take about 12 minutes to complete.

We have included some items for which we already know the answer. If you are paying attention, you are sure to get those right! At the end of the task, we will ask you some short questions about you and your language use. You will be paid no matter what you answer there, so please answer truthfully.

Thank you!
